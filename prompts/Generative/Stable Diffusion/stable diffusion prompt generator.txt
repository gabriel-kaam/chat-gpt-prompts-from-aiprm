If the style is unspecified, it will lean towards photorealism.

Specifics: Specify what you want clearly. If you want a specific composition, say so, such as portrait, headshot, ultrawide shot, extreme closeup, macro shot, an expansive view of.

Singular Nouns or Specific Numbers: Use singular nouns or specific numbers instead of vague plural words.

Combination: Try taking two well-defined concepts and combining them in ways.

Extrapolation: Avoid concepts that involve significant extrapolation, such as "an optimistic vision of an augmented reality future" or "Clothes humans will wear 12,000 years into the future".

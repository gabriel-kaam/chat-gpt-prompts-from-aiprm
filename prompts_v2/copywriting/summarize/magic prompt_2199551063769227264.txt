📌 Magic Prompt Formula Structure

A quality prompt contains these four key parts:

Context: Specifies who or what the AI should act as.

Task: Describes the task to be performed.

Instruction: Details the manner or style of execution (tone, style, granularity).

Data: Provides detailed facts, sample inputs, or specific examples to be used.

Data: The Bluetooth speaker is waterproof, has a 12-hour battery life, and is compact enough to fit in a backpack. It’s perfect for outdoor activities like hiking, picnics, and beach trips.

This structure is versatile and suitable for a broad range of products or services.

ANTI “AI-SCENT” RULES (MANDATORY)
- De-poeticize: rewrite branding/poetic lines into plain language while keeping meaning.
- Remove “guide voice” when it adds no meaning (example: “In this guide…”, “The truth is…”, “Please note…”).

- Rhetorical questions: keep only if they already exist and help; do not add new ones.
- Reduce list-bloat: if FORMATPREFERENCE allows, convert obvious bullet-spam into clean sentences WITHOUT deleting items or changing their order.
- Fix grammar/punctuation, remove repetition, and keep the stronger line when two lines repeat the same idea.

SEO (ONLY IF PRIMARYKEYWORD EXISTS)

FORMATTING RULES
- Keep formatting: do not add/rename headings; preserve list/table order; you may split long paragraphs.
- Minimal formatting: clean paragraphs; keep only necessary lists; remove needless sub-numbering while keeping content.
- Add headings: Markdown headings (##/###) only for Blog/Other; max 6 new headings; never add headings for Email/Script.

smart (unless part of a product/brand name in the input);
quiet; quietly.

# CONCISE Web Page Content Generator: "Tools I Use"

## ROLE & GOAL
Act as a Senior Content Strategist and Copywriter. Your goal is to generate the complete, professional content for a "Tools I Use" web page, outputting the entire result in **[TARGETLANGUAGE]**.

## CONTEXT & INSTRUCTIONS
The content must showcase efficiency and quality, targeting a professional audience. The user's specific request is: **[PROMPT]**.

## FORMAT & CONSTRAINTS
Use **Markdown** throughout.
1. **H1 Title:** Use the variable: **[Page Title (H1)]**
2. **Introduction:** A 3-4 sentence paragraph on your tool selection philosophy.
3. **Sections:** Use **H2** (##) for main groupings (e.g., Core Workflow).
4. **Categories:** Use **H3** (###) for specific tool categories.
5. **Tool Listing:** For every tool, list the **Name (Bolded)** followed by a concise, one-sentence **Justification** for its use.
6. **Conclusion:** A 2-3 sentence professional closing.
7. **Tone:** Professional and authoritative.

## REQUIRED CATEGORIES
The following categories must be used (you may add one more if needed):
* Writing & Content Creation Tools
* AI & Automation Tools
* Social Media Management & Analytics
* Design & Visual Asset Creation
* Project Management & Collaboration
* Development & Infrastructure

## CUSTOMIZATION VARIABLES (Replace all bracketed text)
* **[Your Role/Expertise]:** e.g., Digital Marketer, Full-Stack Developer.
* **[Page Title (H1)]:** e.g., The Essential Workflow Toolkit.
* **[Writing & Content Tools]:** List your 3-5 tools (e.g., Google Docs, Grammarly, Hemingway).
* **[Social Media Tools]:** List your 3-5 tools (e.g., Buffer, Sprout Social).
* **[AI & Automation Tools]:** List your 3-5 tools (e.g., ChatGPT-4, Zapier, Otter.ai).
* **[Other Key Tools]:** List 5-8 tools for the remaining categories (e.g., Figma, ClickUp, Webflow, Google Analytics).

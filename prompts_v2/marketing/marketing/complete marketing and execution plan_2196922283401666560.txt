# 🎯 MARKETING & EXECUTION PLAN

## 1. BUSINESS SNAPSHOT
- **What they sell:** (restate clearly in one line)
- **Who they serve:** (identify 2-3 specific buyer personas with demographics, pain points, and motivations)
- **Market positioning:** (where they sit in the market — premium, mid-market, budget, niche specialist, etc.)
- **Unique value angle:** (what makes this different — infer the most compelling differentiator)

## 2. TARGET AUDIENCE DEEP DIVE
For each persona identified above, provide:
- **Demographics:** Age range, location, income level, job title/role
- **Psychographics:** What they value, what frustrates them, what they aspire to
- **Where they spend time online:** Specific platforms, communities, forums, publications
- **Buying triggers:** What events or pain points push them to seek a solution
- **Objections:** The top 3 reasons they would hesitate to buy

## 3. BRAND MESSAGING FRAMEWORK
- **One-liner:** A single sentence that explains what you do and why it matters
- **Elevator pitch:** A 30-second version (3-4 sentences)
- **Key messages:** 3-5 core messages that should appear across all marketing
- **Proof points:** What evidence, data, or stories support each message
- **Tone of voice:** How the brand should sound (with 3 "we are / we are not" examples)

## 4. MARKETING CHANNEL STRATEGY
Rank the following channels from highest to lowest priority for THIS specific business. For the top 5 channels, provide a specific tactical recommendation:

- Content marketing / SEO
- Email marketing
- Social media (specify which platforms)
- Paid advertising (specify which platforms)
- Partnerships / affiliates
- Community building
- PR / media outreach
- Events / webinars
- Referral / word-of-mouth
- Direct outreach / cold email

For each of the top 5, include:
- **Why this channel:** (specific reason it fits this business)
- **What to do:** (exact tactic, not vague advice)
- **Expected timeline to results:** (realistic)
- **Budget range:** (monthly, if applicable)

## 5. CONTENT STRATEGY
- **Content pillars:** 3-4 core themes to create content around
- **Content types:** Blog posts, videos, podcasts, infographics, tools — which ones and why
- **Publishing cadence:** How often and where
- **10 blog post / content ideas:** Specific titles tied to search intent or audience pain points
- **Lead magnet idea:** One high-value free resource to capture emails

## 6. EMAIL MARKETING PLAN
- **List building strategy:** How to grow the email list from zero
- **Welcome sequence:** 5-email sequence outline (subject line + one-sentence summary for each)
- **Ongoing cadence:** What to send and how often
- **Segmentation strategy:** How to segment as the list grows
- **Key automation triggers:** What events should trigger automated emails

- **5 post ideas:** Specific post concepts for the primary platform
- **Growth tactics:** 3 specific tactics to grow followers organically

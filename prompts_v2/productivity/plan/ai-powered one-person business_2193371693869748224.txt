FOR EACH OF THE 15 IDEAS, use this exact structure:

4. **Core Offer** — Choose one primary offer type:

5. **Revenue Model**

6. **Distribution Strategy (Solo-Friendly)**

7. **AI and Automation Leverage**

   - What manual work it replaces

8. **Setup Complexity**
